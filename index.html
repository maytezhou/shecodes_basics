I ❤️ Dolphins 🐬

If you don't, don't worry. You'll love them soon💞

I was born in Pucallpa, a city located in the eastern part of Peru .
Growing up I used to go to the lakeshore to contemplate these wonderful creatures.
I remember seeing them jumping and playing around in the water.
I also remember a beautiful melody they make.
Hearing them always brought me so much peace and warm up my heart🥰.

Here are some interesting facts about them:

  • They are very intelligent, talented mimics and quick learners 🤓.
  • They are highly social, show empathy and emotions 😊.
  • They use tools to solve complex tasks⚒️.
